BLACKPINK (블랙핑크)

South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members JISOO, JENNIE, ROSÉ, and LISA.

The group debuted in August 2016 with their single album Square One, which featured "Whistle" and "Boombayah".

Over the past 7 years, the group has released 2 studio albums, 4 live albums, 4 single albums and 3 EPs. Each member has also released their individual solo albums.

Their loyal fans are known as BLINKS and similarly the members of the group are referred to as the PINKS.
